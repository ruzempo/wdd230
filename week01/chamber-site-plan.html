Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the GAM Companies Chamber of Commerce is to promote the regional economy and add value to the community of businessmen, entrepreneurs and artisans of the Gustavo A. Madero mayor's office. We promote business through member services, economic and community development, we also teach business courses to benefit the economy of our community.

Audience

Our target audience is people from 18 to 90 years old, people who own companies, micro-enterprises or ongoing ventures. People who want to increase their business knowledge, contact other companies and support other entrepreneurs. Access to our website must be by desktop, laptop or mobile device..

Scenarios

How can I contact more clients? How can I learn more about running my business? Who can grant me a loan to invest in my business? How to improve the finances of my company? What upcoming events will the chamber hold to promote entrepreneurship?
